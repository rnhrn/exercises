DEADLIFT
  • Stand on the resistance band with your feet hip-width apart.
  • Hold the handles or loops of the resistance band in each hand, palms facing your body.
  • Keeping your back straight and knees slightly bent, hinge at the hips and lower your torso towards the ground while keeping the resistance band close to your body.
  • Engage your hamstrings and glutes to return to the starting position. Repeat for 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
