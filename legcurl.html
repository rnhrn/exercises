LEG CURLS
  • sit down with the resistance band looped around your ankles and anchored to a low, sturdy object behind you (video shows in front).
  • Keeping your hips flat on the ground, bend your knees and pull your heels towards your glutes against the resistance of the band.
  • Slowly straighten your legs back to the starting position. Repeat for 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
